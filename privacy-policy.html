Privacy Policy

This Calculator web app is a client-side application that performs basic arithmetic operations directly in your browser.

No personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted by this application. All calculations happen locally on your device, and no information is sent to any server.

There is no use of cookies, analytics tools, third-party scripts, or data tracking of any kind.

If you have any questions about this policy, feel free to reach out through the GitHub repository where this app is hosted.
